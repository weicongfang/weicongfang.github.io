<section class="academic-section academic-section--intro" aria-labelledby="research-focus-title">
  <p class="eyebrow">Research focus</p>
  <h2 id="research-focus-title">Communication in uncertain and AI-mediated information environments</h2>
  <p class="section-lead">My work examines how people assess sources, messages, and support when information is contested or uncertain. I am particularly interested in the roles that generative AI may play in health-related information seeking, trust, judgment, and help-seeking.</p>
  <div class="interest-grid interest-grid--compact">
    <article class="interest-card">
      <h3>Misinformation Correction</h3>
      <p>Fact-checking sources, corrective strategies, reasoning order, and audience responses.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="interest-card">
      <h3>Human-AI Communication</h3>
      <p>Human, AI, and hybrid source identities, as well as interactions with generative AI.</p>
    </article>
    <article class="interest-card">
      <h3>Health Communication</h3>
      <p>Information and support under health uncertainty, including implications for well-being.</p>
    </article>
  </div>
  <p class="methods-line"><strong>Methods:</strong> experiments, surveys, computational text analysis, social network analysis, sentiment analysis, and thematic analysis.</p>
</section>
